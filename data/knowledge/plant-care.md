# Basic Plant Care Guide

## Lavender Care

Lavender prefers full sun and well-drained soil.
Water deeply but infrequently, and allow the soil to dry between watering cycles.

## Snake Plant Care

Snake plants tolerate partial shade and low watering frequency.
Overwatering is the most common cause of root damage.

## Balcony Gardening Tips

Use containers with drainage holes and refresh potting soil each season.
For sunny balconies, choose beginner-friendly species such as lavender and geranium.
