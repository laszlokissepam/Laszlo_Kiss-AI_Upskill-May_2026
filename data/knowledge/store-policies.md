# Green Oasis Store Policies

## Opening Hours

We are open Monday to Friday from 08:00 to 18:00 and Saturday from 09:00 to 16:00.
The store is closed on Sunday.

## Payment Methods

We accept cash, debit cards, and major credit cards.
Gift cards issued by Green Oasis are also accepted.

## Delivery Policy

Home delivery is available for in-stock items within 30 km of the store.
Delivery usually takes 1 to 3 business days depending on item size and stock location.

## Returns Policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days with receipt and original packaging.
Live plants can be returned only if they were damaged at purchase.
